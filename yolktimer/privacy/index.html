Yolk Timer Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Yolk Timer is designed to be simple and respectful of your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

Yolk Timer does not collect, store, or process personal data. The app does not require account registration and does not collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or device identifiers.

How We Use Information

The app does not use personal data for analytics, advertising, or other purposes. Any local settings used by the app are only used to provide the timer experience on your device.

Sharing of Information

Yolk Timer does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. No data is transferred to external servers by the app.

Data Retention

Because the app does not collect personal data, there is no personal information retained by the app or by us.

Children’s Privacy

Yolk Timer is not intended for children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at petr@holub.dev.
